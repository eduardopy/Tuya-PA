Why Choose TUYA PA?

Established in 2008, we are a local and completely independent practice specialized in geriatric care. Dr. Guillermo Morel and Dr. Eileen Fermin, board-certified physicians with specialties in Geriatric Medicine and over 20 years of experience, opened our first office in Sebastian. In 2021, the practice added a second office, located in Vero Beach.

Services

Chronic Care Management

Our Chronic Care Manegement program is focused on Medicare patients with chronic conditions such as arthritis, diabetes or depression. Monthly phone calls with a provider allow us to more effectively assist our patients in managing these conditions through a detailed care plan and better coordination of care with specialists.

Preventive Care

Our preventive care service focuses on health promotion and disease prevention. Through regular check-ups and screenings, we aim to prevent illnesses before they start or detect them at their earliest stages.

Comprehensive Geriatric Care

We take pride in providing outstanding care for patients aged 65 and older. Our focus goes beyond just managing conditions; we are committed to a prevention-based approach that empowers our patients to maintain the best possible quality of life.

Prescription Management

Our prescription management service ensures safe and effective medication use. We monitor your prescriptions to avoid drug interactions, manage refills, and ensure that medications are achieving their intended health benefits.

Primary Care

Our service caters to the general healthcare needs of the patients. It includes annual physical exams, routine health screenings, immunizations, and treatment of acute illnesses. Primary care physicians also coordinate with specialists for conditions that require more targeted treatments.

Patient-Focused Approach

We do more than just manage your health; we care for you like family. With proactive appointment reminders, thoughtful follow-ups, and tailored care plans, we ensure every interaction is centered on your well-being. Our commitment is to make your healthcare journey as comfortable as possible, focusing on you, the patient, every step of the way.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to common questions about our practice. For anything not covered, feel free to reach out to us.

  • Which insurance plans do you accept?

    Our practice accepts Medicare, United Health, Wellmed, Devoted, and Humana. For the most current and comprehensive list, please refer to the 'Insurance Information' section on our website.

  • How can I reach out if I need to contact my doctor?

    We encourage you to send non-emergency messages to your doctor via our secure online patient portal. Our staff diligently checks and responds to these messages within one business day.

  • How do I get a prescription refill?

    Please submit your prescription refill request through our secure online patient portal. We'll process your request promptly and notify you once it's ready.

  • Can I view my medical records online?

    Absolutely, our online patient portal gives you secure access to your medical records, allowing you to view test results, appointment summaries, and more at your convenience.

  • Do you offer telehealth services?

    Yes, we do. For those who prefer or require virtual consultations, we offer telehealth appointments.
